About the Founder

Founder Vision

The late Mr. Abdulla Ali Bin Haidar

(1926 - 1985). Founder of Abdulla Ali Bin Haidar Group

More than 50 years ago, life in Dubai was based on hunting and pearl diving in the Arabian Gulf, and some trading businesses. Resources were hardly available and living conditions were harsh. Despite all these difficulties, the late Mr. Abdulla Ali Bin Haidar had the vision to see this as a challenge for him to find new resources and opportunities to reach a better life. He began looking for other ways to earn a living in that period. His precious little asset was a little of the experience that he gained from his father.

The late Mr. Abdulla Ali Bin Haidar, with his skill and careful analysis of the business reality at that time, was able to take advantage of the opportunity to establish a specialized workshop in automotive mechanics in 1972.

In 1975, the first company of the Bin Haidar Group was established, by the name of Bin Haidar Transport Company. As the business progressed, it gradually led to the establishment of the second company of Bin Haidar Group in the name of Abdulla Bin Haidar General Trading Company (1978). In 1982, the United Arab Emirates Mechanical Lathe for Heavy Machinery became the third company of the Bin Haidar Group.

After these successes and progressive endeavours, the late Mr. Abdulla Ali Bin Haidar was able to establish the nucleus of Ben Haidar Group.

There was also the purchase of land and the construction of warehouses in Dubai and Sharjah, before he passed away after his worldly endeavours, in God's mercy, in 1985.
