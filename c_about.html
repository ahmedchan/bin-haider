About the Company

Al Qahira has established itself as a significant entity in the area of heavy equipment and heavy duty diesel engines. It caters to a growing market for these, among industrial and commercial users. Al Qahira continues to serve and develop this sector of the market, by sourcing the best quality products at competitive prices, while ensuring that no compromise is made on quality. The company has established excellent client relations over the years, and caters to their needs effectively through an efficient network of distributors. Al Qahira adopts innovative operating practices that give it a distinctive edge over its competitors. Support and ancillary services that clients may need, on occasion, are also catered to, with thorough professionalism.

Al Qahira is Contracting Company is an ISO 9001:2008/ OHSAS 18001:2007 / ISO 14001:2004 certified company, established in 1978. We specialize and excel in the construction industry and have been successful in keeping our lead in this highly competitive sector. Originality and modernity are our defining strengths. These sterling qualities help focus our innovative initiatives in the construction of buildings that go on to become distinguished landmarks in the skylines that they grace.
